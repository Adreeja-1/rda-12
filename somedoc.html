05 : About Us

Squid Games were organized in an attempt to help the people in debt. The premise of the game is that a group of players play popular children's games against each other with an ultimate motive to win. There is only one winner and the winner gets a prize amount of $2 billion. The money in the big piggy jar gets filled with a significant amount(the amount that a particular player owes in total) each time a player gets eliminated.
